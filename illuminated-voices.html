Illuminated Voices

This is a prototype of a project that shows its functionality. Imagine that the green LED pin light is a strand of white LED lights installed behind the frame of the shadow box, illuminating the text that is hard to read in the cut-out stencil at the forefront of the box; and the wires are soldered in place, the arduino is neatly secured to the back of the frame. Finally, imagine there is one wire connecting the hand to the box. This project does require power.
