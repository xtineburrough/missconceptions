Bio

xtine burrough is a media artist.

She uses remix as a strategy for engaging networked audiences in critical participation at the intersection of computational art, social engagement, and digital poetry. Longer bio...

On Campus

A professor in the Bass School of Arts, Humanities and Technology at The University of Texas at Dallas, burrough was Area Head of Design + Creative Practice 2019-2022. She teaches courses in media art and activism, social practice, and computational art; and organizes the Pop-up Locker Exhibition with honors students.

LabSynthE

burrough is Director of LabSynthE, a framework for collaboration in the Bass School of Arts, Humanities, and Technology. LabSynthE is a laboratory for the creative development of synthetic & electronic poetry.
